Company Profile

Casina Engineering, founded in 1988, specializes in manufacturing both custom and serial parts using a variety of advanced machining techniques including CNC machining, CNC turning, sheet metal fabrication, and 3D printing. Our platform streamlines the quotation process, making it quick and easy for product designers and engineers to find cost-effective manufacturing solutions tailored to their specific needs. Casina Engineering is dedicated to providing fast, high-quality manufacturing services to clients worldwide.

FAQ's

What materials are commonly used in die manufacturing ?

Common materials used in die manufacturing include steel, aluminum, carbide, and various alloys, depending on the application and requirements.

What types of dies do you offer ?

We offer a wide range of dies, including stamping dies, forging dies, extrusion dies, and cutting dies, among others, tailored to suit different industrial needs.

How can I determine the right type of die for my project ?

Our team of experts is available to assist you in selecting the most suitable die for your specific application. Factors such as material, production volume, and desired output quality are considered to ensure optimal performance.

Can you provide custom die designs ?

Yes, we specialize in providing custom die designs tailored to meet your unique requirements. Our experienced engineers work closely with you to develop innovative solutions that optimize performance and efficiency.
