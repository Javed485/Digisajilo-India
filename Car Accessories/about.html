Our History

A.V Car Sringar

A.V Car Sringar lists products from renowned and established brands that already guarantee excellent quality. Most of the auto accessories and parts listed have undergone strict quality control audits. Furthermore, the quality assurance team at A.V Car Sringar makes sure that only genuine products are delivered to the customers.
It all started when a young bike enthusiast realized that the Indian automobile market was not offering the best variety of auto parts that our cars and bikes deserved. That is when Sanjay Kumar, the founder, and CEO of A.V Car Sringar, came up with the idea of building a one-stop platform for every kind of auto accessories and parts - an online platform that hosts the widest variety of auto accessories and parts and a platform that everyone can trust.
