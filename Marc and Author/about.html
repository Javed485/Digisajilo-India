ABOUT COMPANY
We are Best In Interior
M/s Marc & Author is a leading Service provider for Interiors, Technology, Civil, Fabrication, Engineering & Turn Key related services.

We have been providing Engineering, Fabrication, Civil & Turn Key Solutions to our esteemed Retail, Corporate & Institutional Clients for almost two decades and have recently diversified our expertise to the Interior & Turn Key Solutions for our Esteemed Clients in Residential, Retail, Commercial, Institutional & Banking sector. Marc & Author, in recent past has provided it’s Interior Services to Red Tape, Oriflame, Shyam Telecom, ICICI Bank, HDFC Bank and Kotak Mahindra Bank & various other Retail companies for their Retail & Office space related requirements by setting up their E-Lobbies, ATM’s, Branches & Renovation Projects in Gurgaon, Hisar, Karnal, Azadpur and Kalkaji in the States of Haryana & Delhi.

We assure our clients prompt & quality services for all Interior, Access, Security & Surveillance, Engineering, Electrical, Lighting & Turn Key service requirements in upcoming Residential, Office & Retail Space for new as well as renovation projects
