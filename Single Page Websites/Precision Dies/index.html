About Our Firm

Founded in the year 1995, we “Precision Moulds & Dies” are reliable and prominent manufacturer and supplier of a remarkable range of Syringe Gasket Mould, Plunger Mould, Hose Mould, Casting Dies, Injection Mould, etc. We are a Sole Proprietorship Enterprise that is located at Faridabad (Haryana, India) and constructed a wide and ultramodern infrastructural unit. We have also selected a team of deft and committed professionals who play the most vital role in the expansion of our company. Under the fruitful direction of our Proprietor “Mr. Balvinder Singh”, we have gained huge clientele across the nation. Moreover, we also provide CAD Designing Facility Services at most nominal rates.
