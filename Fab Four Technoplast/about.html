About Fab Four Technoplast

Long-lasting energy for lasting memories.

In 2022, Fab Four Industries, a leading plastic injection moulding company was established by a group of Four Fabulous friends based in Rudrapur and Kashipur. Fab Four was born to meet the demand for industrial battery containers & accessories. Thereafter it has seen steady growth in business & is considered to be one of the top 10 plastic injection moulding companies. Fab Four supplies battery containers to leading battery manufacturers in India and it has been continuously adding prestigious satisfied customers with demanding critical moulding needs. We offer you a comprehensive service & solution for injection moulding of engineering plastic components with part weights up to 5 kg.
Our Company Process

Plastic Injection Moulding Process

Our injection moulding plant is equipped with a variety of modern high precision conventional injection moulding machines in the 250 Ton to the 775-ton range and supporting equipment like MTC. All moulded products are processed using reliable moulding principles with the best-experienced operators, ensuring the best quality possible. Whether you need 25 pieces or a full 24 hours/365 days of production, we have you covered with the best injection moulding cost. With an eye for fine details, we take care of every aspect of moulding. Often, we hear questions like, What causes bubbles in plastic injection moulding? Moisture in the raw material causes bubbles so we ensure pre-heating before moulding to avoid such defects.
