Welcome to Fab Four Technoplast

We are the Manufacturer of Plastic Injection Moulded Parts

FAB FOUR TECHNOPLAST , an ISO 9001:2015 certified company, is one of the leading manufacturers and exporters of Plastic Injection Molded parts in India . In 2022, Fab Four Industries, a leading plastic injection moulding company was established by a group of Four Fabulous friends based in Rudrapur and Kashipur. Fab Four was born to meet the demand for industrial battery containers & accessories. Thereafter it has seen steady growth in business & is considered to be one of the top 10 plastic injection moulding companies.
