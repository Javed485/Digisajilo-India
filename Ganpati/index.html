Our Group Started business of in 1996 as name & style of “ M/s GANPATI ENTERPRISES” under the proprietorship of our group founder Sri RAKESH KUMAR SINGH.Main nature of business is trading of COMPUTER and their products. After that our company started to do business with CENTRAL COAL Limited (CCL) under the authorized AMC concept till 2001. we got MOU with CCL for SUPPLYING AND AMC of COMPUTER AND ACCESSORIES.
