About Company

Makeup services have become some of the top requirements in our everyday life. It's more than just a necessity. Studio A1 doesn't just take care of the customer's comfort but also the entire consumer experience while visiting for services. Founded by Mrs Mamta Jha, Studio A1 started as a single outlet specializing in salon services and today, has become a trusted destination for beauty and wellness having Spa, Salon, Skincare, Nail, Bridal makeup and Clinic services under one roof. With 5 outlets across India, Studio A1 dedicated team of doctors, therapists, stylists and consultants are there to help you at every step of the service and guide you throughout your journey.

Hair Makeup

Facial

Our best services

Hair Treatment

Hair treatment is a range of services that aim to improve the condition and health of the hair. It includes various techniques and procedures to nourish, repair, and revitalize hair that has been damaged by environmental factors, chemical treatments, or daily wear and tear.

Skin Treatment

Skin treatment refers to various procedures and techniques used to improve the appearance and health of the skin. Skin treatments can be done for various reasons, including reducing the signs of aging, improving skin texture and tone, treating acne.

Home Service

Home services in a beauty parlour refer to the salon services provided by the professionals at the client's home instead of the beauty parlour. This service can be a convenient option for clients who have busy schedules or may not be able to visit the beauty parlour.

Our Team

Our team members in a beauty parlour are the experienced professionals who are trained to provide a range of services to enhance and maintain the beauty and well-being of our clients.
